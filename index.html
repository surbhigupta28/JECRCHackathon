Rules and Regulation

By entering this Contest, you agree to be bound by these Official Contest Rules

IT Hackathon will be a 24 hours hackathon

Students will ﬁnish building their projects and submit them to the online portals. A panel of judges will decide on basis of the submissions.

Prizes will be awarded based on the project’s creativity, execution and impact made to the target users or the community

Participants can choose any platform for development.

Students are requested to bring their own laptops and equipments required at the event.

We will accept applications from a team of 3 students.

40 percent marking will be on proper idea representation.

30 percent marking will be for UI/UX design.And 30 percent marking will on completion of the project.
